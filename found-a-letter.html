Found a letter

In the month of July, I and Rahul visited a book fair. After the fair, we went to have a milkshake and were thrilled to find a letter apparently dated February 16 tucked inside the pages of The God Delusion by Richard Dawkins. We thought it was the 16th of January, but we were wrong. I already posted it on X, but found it interesting enough to dedicate a blog post to it.
