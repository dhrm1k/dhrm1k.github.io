PromoCraft at LJIV

Well, what were you doing this Valentine's? What we were doing was presenting promocraft.xyz at LJ Innovation Village.

It was an experience. For those who haven't had the time to understand what we’re building, here's a brief explanation:

With PromoCraft, we are streamlining the process of creating promotional material. Right now, if you want to design promotional content for your business, the workflow looks something like this:

Current process:
Google → Canva → Search the entire web for a great background → Ensure there's no watermark → Edit it → Add text → Remove background from logos → Add more elements.

While Canva is great, it can be frustrating for users who just want something quick and efficient. That’s where PromoCraft comes in.

With PromoCraft:
Google → PromoCraft → Select a template from your collection → Add your logo with our powerful editor → Download the design.

PromoCraft provides beautifully curated templates that you can customize with ease, all within one app. Say goodbye to the hassle and hello to instant, high-quality promotional designs!
